Finn Whitla
Artist Bio ―

Helping create Rose Quartz Festival & rinsin’ techno in Geelong from 2002 until a move to Hobart, the picturesque hoons that Finn brings are well placed in Tasmania. With Tornado Wallace, Andy Hart, Katie Drover, Mike Callander & more crossing the Bass Strait the hoon heat has been high!
