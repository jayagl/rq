About ―

Welcome to Rose Quartz festival; a sensory escape in the wilderness of southwestern Tasmania.

Three days and two wild nights. Nature, scenery, isolation. Dancing, dreaming and all the rest of it, on the shores of the vast Lake Pedder. A curated selection of the best musical and visual artists from Australia and beyond. And with no more than 500 tickets available, we’re keeping it cosy; just you, mother nature, and the best vibes anyone could muster this side of the equator.
